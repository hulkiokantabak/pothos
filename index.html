POTHOS — a garden of appointments

Hold and let go — or press once to take it and once to let go.

Sow a seed

Plant a seed. It names the exact day it will open — a day away, a week, a season — and nothing you do can hurry it.

Hold a seed and let go — or press once to take it and once to let go. A longer hold sows a taller plant. There is no skill in it. What grows cannot be helped, hurried, or harmed. It will tell you exactly when it opens; the bloom is brief, and if you are away while it is open, the record will say so.

The garden

Nothing is sown here. A garden lives only in the browser that sowed it — and some browsers forget it after a week or two away. If you sowed one here and kept its seed line, or a saved copy of the garden, opening that brings the plant back, still keeping its appointments.
